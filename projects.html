BlueComputer — Local-First Course-Specific AI Tutor

A local-first AI tutor that answers only course-relevant questions for K–12 learning activities. Fine-tuned language models with Apple’s MLX framework and integrated Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) plus an on/off-topic classifier to keep responses aligned with the lesson. Optimized prompt flow and inference to achieve sub-8s average response times in an end-to-end SwiftUI experience.

Focus: MLX fine-tuning, NLP evaluation, RAG, routing/classification, SwiftUI product integration.
